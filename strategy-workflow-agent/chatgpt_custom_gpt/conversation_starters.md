# Conversation Starters for Strategic Planning Workflow Agent

These conversation starters help users quickly engage with the Custom GPT for various strategic planning needs.

## Primary Starters

### 1. Full Strategic Plan
**Prompt**: "I want to create a comprehensive 3-year strategic plan for my organization. Guide me through the process."

**Expected Flow**: Full 11-phase workflow from vision extraction through feedback loop.

---

### 2. Talent Assessment Strategy
**Prompt**: "Help me develop a global talent assessment strategy with clear use cases, governance, and ROI projections."

**Expected Flow**: Specialized workflow focused on:
- Talent assessment use cases (hiring, development, succession)
- Assessment tool portfolio
- Governance framework
- Compliance considerations
- ROI metrics

---

### 3. Strategic Analysis & Pillars
**Prompt**: "Analyze our current strategic position and help develop 3-5 strategic pillars with supporting goals."

**Expected Flow**: Phases 1-4:
- Vision extraction
- Context analysis (SWOT, gaps, trends)
- Pillar synthesis
- SMART goal generation

---

### 4. Risk Assessment
**Prompt**: "Perform a comprehensive risk assessment for our strategic initiatives and develop mitigation strategies."

**Expected Flow**: Risk-focused workflow:
- Initiative inventory
- Risk identification across all categories
- Likelihood and impact assessment
- Mitigation strategy development
- Contingency planning

---

### 5. Implementation Roadmap
**Prompt**: "Create an optimized implementation roadmap with resource allocation and timeline for our strategy."

**Expected Flow**: Phases 7-8:
- Resource planning (budget, FTE)
- Timeline optimization
- Critical path analysis
- Milestone scheduling

---

### 6. Executive Summary
**Prompt**: "Generate an executive summary and presentation outline for our strategic plan."

**Expected Flow**: Output generation phase:
- Executive summary document
- Presentation deck outline
- Key messages and visualizations

---

## Quick Actions

### Resume Previous Session
**Prompt**: "Where were we in the strategic planning workflow? Let's continue from where we left off."

### Quality Check
**Prompt**: "Review the quality scores for our current strategy and identify areas for improvement."

### Get Feedback
**Prompt**: "What did you learn from this planning session? How can we improve the outputs?"

### Export Outputs
**Prompt**: "Generate all deliverables for our strategic plan in a format ready for stakeholder presentation."

---

## Tips for Users

1. **Provide Context**: The more background you share, the better the outputs.

2. **Be Specific**: Instead of "create a strategy," specify industry, timeframe, and focus areas.

3. **Iterate**: Don't hesitate to ask for revisions or explore alternatives.

4. **Use Phases**: If you only need part of the workflow, specify which phases.

5. **Give Feedback**: Rating outputs helps the system improve over time.
